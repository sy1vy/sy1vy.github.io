Lycoris Recoil (リコリス・リコイル) is a Japanese mixed-media project created by Spider Lily and Asaura.


The original anime television series, animated and produced by A-1 Pictures began airing in July 2022. A manga adaptation by Yasunori Bizen began serialization in September 2022. A spin-off light novel released in the same month, written by Asaura and illustrated by Imigimuru.
Synopsis




For these peaceful days - there’s a secret behind it all. A secret organization that prevents crimes: DA - Direct Attack, and their group of all-girl agents - Lycoris. This peaceful everyday life is all thanks to these young girls.


The elite Chisato Nishikigi is the strongest Lycoris agent of all time. Alongside her is her new partner, Takina Inoue, the talented but mysterious Lycoris. They work together at one of the DA's branches – Café LycoReco.


Watching over them is Chisato's mysterious former teacher and café manager, Mika and a young woman constantly on the lookout for a husband, Mizuki Nakahara. Working alongside them is a petite girl with a storied past and an extraordinary set of skills, Kurumi.


Here, the orders this café don't stop with coffee and sweets. From childcare and shopping to thwarting assassinations and investigating arms deals, at Café LycoReco no two days are the same.


The free-spirited and optimistic mercenary with a heart of gold, Chisato. The cool-headed and efficient agent, Takina.


The chaotic everyday lives of the mismatched duo begin!
